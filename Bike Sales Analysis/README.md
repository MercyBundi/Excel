# 🚴‍♂️ Bike Sales Analysis Project (Excel)

This project focuses on analyzing bike sales data using Microsoft Excel to uncover key business insights, customer trends, and purchasing behavior. The analysis was performed entirely using Excel tools such as Pivot Tables, Charts, Filters, and Data Cleaning functions.

## 📊 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to explore bike sales data and identify factors that influence customer purchasing decisions. The analysis helps answer questions such as:

* Which customer groups are most likely to purchase bikes?
* How do income levels affect bike purchases?
* What role do age, gender, and region play in sales trends?

![Bike Sales Dashboard pic](https://github.com/MercyBundi/Excel/blob/main/Bike%20Sales%20Analysis/Bike%20Sales%20Dashboard%20pic.png)

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and prepared in Excel by:

* Removing duplicates
* Handling missing values
* Standardizing categorical values (e.g., Gender, Marital Status)
* Creating new calculated fields (e.g., Age brackets)
* Formatting data for analysis

## 📈 Analysis Performed

The analysis was conducted using Excel features such as Pivot Tables and Charts:

* Customer segmentation by **age, gender, and income**
* Relationship between **income and bike purchases**
* Impact of **commute distance** on purchase decision
* Analysis of **occupation and education level**
* Regional sales performance comparison
* Family structure (children & marital status) vs purchase behavior

## 📌 Key Insights
* Customers with **higher income levels** were more likely to purchase bikes
* **Middle-aged customers** showed the highest purchase rate
* Customers with **longer commute distances** had a higher likelihood of buying bikes
* Professionals and skilled workers showed stronger purchase tendencies
* Certain regions had significantly higher bike adoption rates
